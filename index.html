Hello, I'm Elena 

I am a UX Researcher, based in Barcelona
but it doesn't matter anymore where you are, right?

My career path in IT started in Israel when I moved to this country and found a job at Wix.com. I came there with almost zero knowledge about websites and left as a team leader of Russian support team.
I love challenging myself and after a few years of working in software companies on different positions that were always quite close to user experience.
I decided to get a deeper knowledge about UX Design. I was always passionate about psychology and understanding users needs and turning this knowledge into business value and happiness of the users.
Currently I am completing my masters degree in UX Design in Barcelona and willing to stay in this amazing city. I am searching for the opportunity to find a way to implement my skills and passion for UX Design.
